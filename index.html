Nutritious Azolla

Our azolla is a highly nutritious aquatic plant, perfect for sustainable livestock feed and enhancing soil fertility.

Country Chicken

Our free-range country chicken is not only rich in flavor but also packed with essential nutrients, making it a healthy choice for your meals. Raised in a natural environment, these chickens roam freely, foraging for insects, seeds, and grasses, which contributes to their unique taste and texture. Unlike commercially raised chickens, our country chickens are allowed to grow at their own pace, resulting in firmer meat that is both tender and flavorful. With minimal exposure to antibiotics and hormones, you can enjoy peace of mind knowing that you are consuming a wholesome product that supports your health and well-being. Whether grilled, roasted, or used in traditional recipes, our country chicken delivers a delicious and nutritious dining experience for you and your family.

Banana Products

Our banana products include fresh bananas, leaves, flowers, and stems, offering nutritious, versatile options for healthy cooking and snacking.

Black Rice Paddy

One of our best products, Black Rice Paddy, also known as "forbidden rice," is a premium grain celebrated for its rich flavor and exceptional nutritional benefits. Packed with antioxidants and fiber, it offers a unique taste and texture that enhances any dish.

Tender Coconut

Tender Coconut, is a refreshing and nutritious beverage that is loved for its naturally sweet taste and hydrating properties. Rich in electrolytes, vitamins, and minerals, it provides an instant energy boost and is perfect for quenching your thirst.
